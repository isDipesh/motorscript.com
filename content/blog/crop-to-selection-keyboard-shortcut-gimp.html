--- title: ‘Crop to Selection’ keyboard shortcut for GIMP created: !!timestamp '2014-11-06 07:10:19' tags: - gimp ---

  1. Enable Use dynamic keyboard shortcuts and Save keyboard shortcuts on exit from Edit -> Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts

  2. From the Image menu, hover over Crop to Selection and hit a three key sequence (like Ctrl + Alt + C).

  3. Rejoice!
